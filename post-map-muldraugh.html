Muldraugh Map Guide: Small Town, Low Risk, Perfect for Beginners

New May 9, 2026 Build 42 ~6 min read

Muldraugh is a small town with low zombie density and basic loot. It is the best starting location for beginners who want to learn the game mechanics without being overwhelmed.

Overview

Muldraugh is a small rural town with a few commercial buildings, residential houses, and a gas station. The zombie population is significantly lower here than in Rosewood or Riverside.

Best Base Locations

LocationSafetyLootNotes
Residential HousesHighLowVery safe, easy to fortify, minimal loot
Gas StationMediumMediumGas cans, snacks, drinks, basic tools
DinerLowMediumFood loot, but more zombies than other areas

Loot Routes

  • Residential Area: Kitchen supplies, canned food, water, clothing.
  • Gas Station: Gas cans, oil, snacks, drinks, basic tools.
  • Diner: Food, plates, cutlery, napkins.

Pro Tip: Muldraugh has limited high-value loot. Once you have gathered the basics, consider traveling to Rosewood or Riverside for better supplies.

Danger Zones

  • Town Center: Slightly higher zombie density, but still manageable for beginners.
  • Main Roads: Some zombie traffic, but generally safe.

Getting to Other Towns

  • To Rosewood: Head north along the main road. About 30 minutes of walking.
  • To West Point: Follow the road west. Approximately 45 minutes of walking.
  • To Riverside: Head east, then north. About 1 hour of walking.

Best For: Absolute beginners, solo players, and low-stress practice runs. Muldraugh is the easiest town to survive in.
