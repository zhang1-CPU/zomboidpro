Build 42.18+ Complete Update Guide

Hot June 20, 2026 Build 42.18+

Build 42.18 and later updates bring significant changes to Project Zomboid. This guide covers all the new features, mechanics, and strategies.

New Features Overview

Animal Taming System

Train dogs as companions, hunt wildlife, and establish a sustainable farm with the new animal husbandry system.

AnimalBehaviorUses
DogsCan be trainedCompanion, early warning
CatsIndependentControls pests
ChickensDomesticatedEgg production

Dynamic Weather System

  • Rain Puddles - Rain creates standing water that can be collected
  • Temperature Effects - High temperatures accelerate food spoilage
  • Wind Direction - Affects fire spread and zombie detection
  • Seasonal Changes - Spring, summer, autumn, winter cycles

Electrical Network Expansion

  • Transformers - Convert high-voltage power
  • Batteries - Store electricity for later use
  • Solar Panels - Generate clean energy during the day
  • Wind Turbines - Generate power from wind

Survival Strategy Updates

  • Early Game: Prioritize finding a dog companion for early warning
  • Mid Game: Establish a farm with animals and crops
  • Late Game: Create a self-sustaining compound with renewable energy
