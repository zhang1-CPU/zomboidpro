Zombie Bite Infection Guide: Exact Rates, Timeline & No Cure

Written for Build 42 2026-05-11 ~6 min read

The infection system in Project Zomboid is unforgiving. Understanding exactly how it works matters ?because once you're infected, there is no cure.

Infection Types ?Not All Wounds Are Equal

Wound TypeKnox Infection RateSeverity
Bite~93%Fatal in almost all cases
Laceration~25%Can be survived, but risky
Scratch~7%Most likely to survive

There is no cure for Knox Infection. Once you are infected, your character will die.

How to Tell if You're Infected

  1. You get bitten or scratched by a zombie.
  2. If the "Queasy" moodle appears 6-12 hours later with no other cause, it's Knox Infection.
  3. Death follows within 2-4 days. There is no cure.

Prevention is Everything

  • Wear protective clothing (jackets, long pants, gloves).
  • Avoid fights you don't need.
  • Know when to run (3+ zombies = leave).
