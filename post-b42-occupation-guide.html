Occupation System

How Occupations Work in Project Zomboid Build 42

Each occupation starts with bonus skill points and unique abilities. Choose based on your survival priorities: combat, utility, or stealth.

Key Point: Occupations cost 0-10 points depending on their starting advantages. More powerful occupations require points, which means taking negative traits. Plan your character build around your occupation choice.

Best for Beginners

Top Occupation Choices for New Players

These occupations provide the strongest early-game advantages with manageable point costs.

Carpenter

Point Cost: 4 points
Starting Bonus: +3 Carpentry
Why Choose Carpenter:

  • Skip the early carpentry grind entirely
  • Build rain collectors and fortifications on Day 1
  • Create sustainable water sources before water shuts off
  • Strong for base-building focused players

Carpenter is the easiest occupation for new players because it solves the water problem immediately. Rain collectors built on Day 1 provide permanent water security.

Fire Officer

Point Cost: 6 points
Starting Bonus: +2 Strength, +1 Fitness, Axe proficiency
Why Choose Fire Officer:

  • Excellent melee combat from the start
  • High Strength means better carry capacity
  • Fire axe is one of the best early weapons
  • Good for aggressive playstyles

Fire Officer balances combat and utility. The axe bonus makes clearing early buildings much safer, while Strength and Fitness provide survival flexibility.

Burglar

Point Cost: 4 points
Starting Bonus: +2 Nimble, +1 Sneaking, Hotwire skill
Why Choose Burglar:

  • Instant vehicle access without keys
  • Excellent stealth capabilities
  • Move silently past zombie hordes
  • Ideal for loot-focused survivors

Burglar excels at avoiding danger entirely. Hotwiring cars provides instant mobility, while high sneak lets you loot dangerous areas safely.

Utility Roles

Best Utility Occupations for Survival

These occupations excel at specific survival tasks beyond combat.

Nurse

Point Cost: 6 points
Starting Bonus: +3 First Aid, +1 Doctor
Why Choose Nurse:

  • Superior wound treatment from start
  • First Aid books have double effect
  • Essential for multiplayer co-op support
  • Treat injuries without equipment penalty

Repairman

Point Cost: 4 points
Starting Bonus: +2 Maintenance, +1 Electrical, +1 Metalworking
Why Choose Repairman:

  • Keep weapons and tools working longer
  • Unlock generators and electrical systems
  • Metalworking for advanced crafting
  • Best for long-term base sustainability

Farmer

Point Cost: 4 points
Starting Bonus: +2 Farming, +1 Plant Identification, +1 Herbalist
Why Choose Farmer:

  • Fast-track sustainable food production
  • Identify edible plants and herbs
  • Perfect for long-term solo survival
  • Best paired with Riverside starting location

Choose Your Occupation and Survive

Each occupation excels at different playstyles. Carpenter and Burglar offer the smoothest early game for beginners. Fire Officer and Nurse provide strong all-around benefits for any strategy.
