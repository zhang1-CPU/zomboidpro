Louisville Map Guide: Surviving the Big City

New June 20, 2026 Build 42

Louisville is the largest and most dangerous city in Project Zomboid. With its massive urban sprawl, high zombie density, and incredible loot potential, Louisville offers both great rewards and extreme risks.

Overview

Louisville is located in the northeastern quadrant of the map. It features skyscrapers, malls, hospitals, police stations, and a military checkpoint.

Key Districts

DistrictRisk LevelLoot Quality
West LouisvilleMediumHigh
DowntownExtremeExtreme
University DistrictHighHigh

Best Base Locations

  • West Louisville Houses - Quiet residential streets
  • University Dorms - Multiple rooms, good defenses
  • Suburban Edge - Houses on the outskirts

High-Value Loot Locations

  • Louisville Mall - Clothing, food, tools, electronics
  • Police Department - Firearms, ammo, body armor
  • General Hospital - Top-tier medical supplies
  • Military Checkpoint - High-grade weapons (extremely dangerous)

Recommended For: Experienced players only. Louisville is not beginner-friendly.
