About This Site

PZ Survival Hub: Free Project Zomboid Guides for Every Player

PZ Survival Hub is a free collection of Project Zomboid guides, maps, tips, and tutorials. We focus on practical, in-game information that helps you survive longer - from your first night to your hundredth day in the apocalypse.

What We Cover

  • Beginner survival tips and your first week walkthrough
  • Map guides for Louisville, Rosewood, Riverside, and Muldraugh
  • Medical decisions, traits, and character builds
  • Base building, farming, and long-term survival planning
  • Build 42.18+ updates, changes, and new feature explanations
  • Weapon comparisons, mod recommendations, and troubleshooting

Who Are Our Guides For?

  • New players starting their first Project Zomboid run
  • Returning players catching up on Build 42 changes
  • Solo survivors looking to extend their character life
  • Co-op teams planning base operations and loot runs
  • Mod users seeking verified recommendations

Our Editorial Approach

  • All guides are written and tested in the current stable build
  • Weekly content updates when Project Zomboid patches release
  • Always free - no paywalls, no subscriptions, no locked content
  • Practical, in-game focused advice rather than theoretical opinions
  • Not affiliated with The Indie Stone - just a community fan site

Frequently Asked Questions About PZ Survival Hub

Are these guides free?

Yes. All guides on PZ Survival Hub are completely free. No sign-up required, no paywalls, no premium content locked behind a subscription. We are supported by ads displayed on our pages.

Are you affiliated with The Indie Stone?

No. PZ Survival Hub is an unofficial fan-created guide site. We love Project Zomboid and create these guides to help other players. All game content, names, and mechanics are trademarks of their respective owners.

How often is content updated?

We update our guides whenever a significant Project Zomboid patch releases. Major Build 42 updates typically get new guide content within days. Our Build 42.18 update guide covers the latest changes.

Can I suggest a guide topic?

Yes! If there's a Project Zomboid topic you'd like a guide for, check our complete guide list first to see if we've already covered it. If not, focus areas include early survival, loot maps, trait builds, and base design.

Do you cover modded gameplay?

We cover both vanilla and popularly-modded gameplay. Our tested mods guide lists mods we've verified to work with the current build and explains what gameplay changes they introduce.

What Build version are these guides for?

All current guides are tested and updated for Project Zomboid Build 42.18 (the latest major stable release). We maintain older build compatibility notes where relevant so you can still follow guides even on older versions.
