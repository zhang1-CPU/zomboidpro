Welcome to PZ Survival Hub

Our Mission

PZ Survival Hub was created to help new and returning players master Project Zomboid. We believe that survival games are most fun when you understand the systems, not when you're repeatedly dying from avoidable mistakes.

What We Offer

We provide practical, no-nonsense guides covering everything from your first night to long-term base building. All content is updated for Build 42 and completely free.

Who We Are

We're a team of dedicated Project Zomboid players who've spent hundreds of hours mastering the game's mechanics. Our guides are based on real gameplay experience, not theory.
