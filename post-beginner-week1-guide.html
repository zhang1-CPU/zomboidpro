Beginner's First Week Guide: Day-by-Day Survival

New May 9, 2026 Build 42 ~7 min read

The first week is the most critical period in Project Zomboid. Make the right decisions and you will have a solid foundation. Make mistakes and you will not survive.

Day 1: Secure Shelter

  • Find a house with few windows (easier to board up).
  • Grab a kitchen knife from the kitchen.
  • Collect food, water, and a bag from nearby houses.
  • Board up all ground-floor windows before dark.
  • Check the TV for weather forecasts.

Day 2-3: Gather Supplies

  • Visit nearby stores for canned food and water.
  • Find a hammer and nails for construction.
  • Start leveling Fitness and Strength by exercising.
  • Scout escape routes from your base.

Day 4-5: Expand and Fortify

  • Build storage crates for organization.
  • Start a small garden near your base.
  • Collect skill books for reading.
  • Check on your water and food stockpiles.

Day 6-7: Long-Term Planning

  • Plan your farming layout.
  • Scout for vehicle parts.
  • Establish a routine for daily tasks.
  • Start preparing for the next season.

Pro Tip: Don't rush. Take your time each day to do things properly. A slow, methodical approach beats a rushed, risky one every time.
