Riverside Map Guide: Large Town, High Risk, High Reward

New May 9, 2026 Build 42 ~8 min read

Riverside is the largest town in the default map. It has excellent loot density, especially for mid-to-late game supplies, but it is also one of the most dangerous areas. This guide covers everything you need to survive Riverside.

Overview

Riverside is a large urban area with dense commercial zones, residential neighborhoods, and industrial districts. The zombie population is higher here, but so is the quality of loot. Best for experienced players or well-prepared groups.

Loot Routes

  • Downtown Commercial: Electronics, clothing, sporting goods, restaurants.
  • Grocery Store: Canned food, fresh food (early game), water, alcohol.
  • Hardware Store: Tools, nails, wood, paint, plumbing supplies.
  • Gas Stations (Multiple): Gas cans, oil, snacks, basic tools.
  • Hospital: Medical supplies, bandages, painkillers. Very dangerous.

Warning: Downtown Riverside has the highest zombie density in the default map. Do not enter without a clear escape plan and at least two weapons.

Danger Zones

  • Downtown Center: Extreme zombie density, open streets, poor cover.
  • Hospital Area: High-value loot, but heavily infested. Go in with a team or not at all.
  • Industrial District: Open areas with poor cover and many zombies.
  • Main Roads: High traffic, lots of zombies. Stick to side streets when possible.

Getting to Other Towns

  • To Rosewood: Follow the river north. About 45 minutes of walking.
  • To West Point: Head southwest along the main road. Approximately 1 hour of walking.
  • To Muldraugh: Head south, then east. About 1.5 hours of walking.

Best For: Experienced players, groups of 2+, and mid-to-late game runs. Riverside rewards preparation and punishes recklessness.
