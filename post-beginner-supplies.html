Beginner Supply Guide 2026: Exact Numbers for Your First Week

Updated May 7, 2026 Build 42 ~8 min read

Most new players either grab too much or not enough. This guide gives you exact numbers for water, food, gas, and essential supplies so you know precisely what to stockpile.

Rule of thumb: One character needs roughly 1 liter of water and 800-1000 calories per day. Plan your stockpiles around these numbers.

Water – Your Top Priority

DurationMinimumRecommendedWhere to Find
3 days3 liters5 litersKitchen sinks (if water still running), bottles in stores
1 week7 liters10 litersSame sources + rain collectors
2 weeks14 liters20 litersAll sources + fill every container you find

Water stops running within a few days to two weeks after the apocalypse starts. Fill every container you can find on Day 1: pots, pans, empty bottles, watering cans, and cooking pots.

Essential Tools

  • Hammer – For construction and barricading. Find in garages, toolboxes, hardware stores.
  • Crowbar – For prying open locked doors. Hardware stores, garages.
  • Knife – Kitchen knife from any house. Your first weapon and tool.
  • Sheet rope – Craft from 3x sheets/blankets. Essential for quick escapes.
  • Bag – Any bag. Increases carry capacity. Check houses, stores, and police stations.

Medical Supplies

  • Bandages – 2-4 minimum. Found in houses, bathrooms, stores.
  • Disinfectant – 1-2 bottles. Prevents infection. Bathrooms, hospitals, clinics.
  • Pills (painkillers) – Optional but helpful. Hospitals, clinics, some houses.

Don't overpack: Carrying too much slows you down and drains stamina. Only grab what you need for the immediate 2-3 days, then expand your stockpile as you secure your base.
