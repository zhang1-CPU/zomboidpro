Build 42 Food & Cooking Guide: From Canned Food to Farming

New May 9, 2026 Build 42 ~7 min read

Food and cooking in Build 42 has evolved significantly. This guide covers everything from basic canned food consumption to advanced farming and preservation techniques.

Food Sources

SourceAvailabilityNotes
Canned FoodHighFound everywhere, no cooking needed, but runs out
Fresh FoodMedium (early game)Fruit, vegetables, bread. Spoils quickly.
FarmingHigh (long-term)Sustainable food source, requires time and skill
TrappingLowRabbits and small animals. Requires traps and skill.
FishingMediumNear rivers and lakes. Requires fishing rod and bait.

Cooking Basics

  1. Find a heat source: Stove, campfire, or grill.
  2. Add ingredients: Right-click the heat source ?Add Ingredient.
  3. Wait for cooking: The food will cook automatically. Don't leave it too long or it will burn.
  4. Remove and eat: Right-click the cooked food and select Eat.

Recipe Tips

  • Simple recipes first: Boiled water + vegetables or meat is a safe starting point.
  • Use spices: Salt and pepper improve happiness and nutrition.
  • Avoid rotten food: Red text means spoiled ?do not eat.
  • Cook in bulk: Save time by cooking multiple meals at once.

Food Preservation

  • Canning: Requires empty jars and a heat source. Sealed food lasts much longer.
  • Drying: Hang meat or fish to dry. Makes jerky that lasts for weeks.
  • Pickling: Use vinegar to preserve vegetables. Requires jars and vinegar.

Pro Tip: Stockpile empty jars and salt early. These are essential for long-term food preservation and are often overlooked by new players.
