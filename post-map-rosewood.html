Rosewood Map Guide: Loot, Base Locations & Danger Zones

New May 9, 2026 Build 42 ~7 min read

Rosewood is one of the most popular starting areas in Project Zomboid. It is small, has good loot density, and is surrounded by forests for easy escapes. This guide covers everything you need to know about surviving in and around Rosewood.

Overview

Rosewood is a medium-sized town with a mix of residential areas, commercial zones, and industrial buildings. It is located in the northeastern part of the map and is an excellent choice for beginners.

Best Base Locations

LocationSafetyLootNotes
Residential Houses (North)HighMediumQuiet, easy to fortify, good for beginners
Fire StationMediumHighExcellent loot (axes, tools, medical supplies), but more zombies
LibraryHighMediumGood for skill books, easy to defend with few entrances
Warehouses (East)LowHighLots of loot, but open and harder to defend

Loot Routes

  • North Residential: Kitchen knives, canned food, water bottles, tools in garages.
  • Downtown Commercial: Electronics, clothing, sporting goods, hardware store.
  • Fire Station: Axes, crowbars, first aid supplies, heavy equipment.
  • Gas Station (West): Gas cans, oil, snacks, drinks, basic tools.

Pro Tip: Rosewood is close to a forest on the east side. Keep your base near the tree line for quick escape routes when zombies swarm.

Danger Zones

  • Downtown Center: High zombie density due to multiple spawn points.
  • Hospital Area: Lots of loot, but heavily infested.
  • Industrial Zone: Open areas with poor cover and many zombies.

Getting to Other Towns

  • To West Point: Head southwest along the main road. Approximately 30 minutes of walking.
  • To Riverside: Follow the river south. About 45 minutes of walking.
  • To Muldraugh: Head south, then west. About 1 hour of walking through forests and fields.

Best For: Beginners, solo players, and small groups. Rosewood offers a balanced mix of loot, safety, and escape options.
